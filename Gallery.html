What We Do
With great pleasure and Jesus joy, I welcome you to this site. The Template is a God-inspired initiative that focuses on creating a platform for the new move of the Holy Spirit in our time - It is a known fact that as time changes, different earthly events, at global, national and communal levels are unfolding posing daunting challenges to our Christian faith and persuasions. In time as this, we need a fresh move of the Holy Spirit in providing insights on how to navigate our path and surmount these challenges. As the unchanging Changer, the Spirit of God has chosen this platform for the release of timely and relevant WORD of life that will - Translate and transform our situations, - Provide the needed wisdom to effectively navigate our changing world - Provide a stand upon which our faith can be built as we grow into the fullness of His image - And ultimately through us creating the needed change in our world as we expect the second return of Our Lord Jesus Christ.
Our Vision
The Template, through the exposé of the word of life, therefore provides the platform for - The move and demonstration of the power of Holy Spirit in this end time - The unveiling of Godly wisdom needed for an effective Christian life in this end time - The faith-base upon which true conviction and genuine Christian persuasions can be built.
Our Commission
In fulfilling our mandate, on the 9th of July 2015, a 15minutes Radio programme titled “The TEMPLATE” started on one of the foremost Broadcasting station in Abeokuta, Ogun state, Nigeria – this provides avenue for the teaching of the WORD, thereby availing understanding into the scriptures. This website is yet another avenue for more people to have access to this word of truth in pursuance of our God-given mandate. Beloved, you have a place - In becoming a part of our Template team member - Contributing to our blog discussions - Getting access and distribution of our messages through the internet and distribution centres - Inviting friends to our websites
